A visualization of Risveglio di una Città

Below is a visualization of the first seven bars of Risveglio di una Città by Luigi Russolo, as performed by Daniele Lombardi. The x-axis represents time, the y-axis represents pitch-space, and the darkness of each block represents magnitude of that component of frequency at that time (black is loudest). The piece is broken into half-second intervals, and columns representing these intervals should appear in real time, every half-second. If not, ensure that JavaScript is enabled. Also, clicking within the plot space will cause a cursor to appear with the approximate note name and time appearing above the plot area (clicking outside the plot area will remove the cursors).
